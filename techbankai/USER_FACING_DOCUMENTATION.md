## TechBank.Ai — User Guide Overview

TechBank.Ai is a talent screening and recruitment platform that helps organizations and candidates connect faster. It organizes resumes, understands job needs, and surfaces the best matches so teams can make confident, consistent hiring decisions with less manual work.

## Who It’s For

- HR teams and recruiters who manage hiring pipelines
- Hiring managers who review shortlists for their teams
- Candidates applying as company employees, freelancers, or guest applicants
- Admins who maintain the talent pool and analytics

## Key Features (Plain Language)

### For Candidates
- Easy resume upload with automatic form pre‑fill
- Simple application form with skills, experience, and education
- Employment‑type selection (company employee, freelancer, guest)
- Profile updates for contact and preference changes
- Optional relocation and notice‑period details

### For Admins and Recruiters
- Central talent pool with searchable candidate records
- Smart matching of candidates to a job description
- Shortlists ranked by fit and explained in plain language
- Bulk resume uploads to grow the talent pool quickly
- Dashboard insights on volume, skills, experience, and locations
- Resume viewing and candidate detail drill‑downs

## How It Works (High‑Level)

1. Candidates submit their resume and details in one flow.
2. The system extracts key information and builds a structured profile.
3. Recruiters create a job description and run a match.
4. The platform returns a ranked list with clear fit reasons.
5. Teams review candidates, open resumes, and proceed to next steps.

## Common Workflows

### Candidate Application Flow
- Sign up or log in
- Choose employment type
- If required, verify ID
- Upload resume (auto‑fills the form)
- Review and submit
- Update profile later if needed

### Admin: Build the Talent Pool
- Upload multiple resumes at once
- Review recently added candidates
- Search by skills, role, location, or experience

### Admin: Match Candidates to a Job
- Upload or paste a job description
- Run matching
- Review top matches with fit reasons
- Open resume and contact details as needed

### Admin: Monitor Hiring Insights
- Track total candidates and trends
- See top skills and experience distribution
- View location distribution for planning

## USPs (What Makes It Different)

- **Fast screening:** Reduces manual resume review time.
- **Clear fit reasons:** Explains why candidates match.
- **Unified database:** All talent is searchable in one place.
- **Flexible intake:** Supports multiple candidate types.
- **Actionable insights:** Makes hiring trends easy to see.

## What Users Can Expect

- A clean, guided experience from upload to submission
- Consistent candidate evaluation across roles
- Quick access to resumes and profiles
- Reliable search and filtering for large talent pools
