## Product Overview

**TechBank.Ai** is an AI-powered talent screening and recruitment platform that revolutionizes how organizations find, evaluate, and match candidates to job opportunities. By leveraging advanced artificial intelligence (GPT-4), the platform automates the time-consuming process of resume screening and candidate matching, enabling HR teams and recruiters to focus on what matters most - building relationships with top talent.

---

## Product Description

TechBank.Ai is a comprehensive talent management solution designed for modern recruitment teams. The platform serves as an intelligent intermediary between job requirements and candidate profiles, using cutting-edge AI to understand context, skills, and experience beyond traditional keyword matching.

### What TechBank.Ai Does

TechBank.Ai transforms the recruitment workflow by:

- **Intelligently Parsing Resumes**: Automatically extracts and structures candidate information from resumes using AI, eliminating manual data entry
- **Analyzing Job Descriptions**: Understands job requirements and extracts key skills, experience levels, and qualifications needed
- **Smart Candidate Matching**: Matches candidates to job openings using a hybrid approach combining keyword matching and AI-powered semantic understanding
- **Centralized Talent Pool**: Maintains a unified database of all candidates including employees, freelancers, and applicants
- **Data-Driven Insights**: Provides analytics and visualizations to understand talent distribution, skills trends, and recruitment patterns
- **Multi-Channel Collection**: Supports resume collection from multiple sources including direct uploads, email integrations (Gmail/Outlook), and bulk imports

The platform is designed to handle multiple user types - Company Employees, Freelancers, Guest Users, and Hired Forces - making it versatile for various recruitment scenarios.
